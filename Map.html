Bagori (Western Range) :

  • Bagori covers an area of approximately 22 kilometers for the Jeep Safari.
  • The Bagori range office is about 300 meters from the National Highway.
  • Approximately 3 to 5 resorts are located near the Bagori range office.

Kohora (Central Range) :

  • Kohora covers an area of approximately 27 kilometers for the Jeep Safari.
  • The Kohora range office is located approximately 2 kilometers from the Naitonal Highway.
  • Approximately 3 to 5 resorts are located near the Kohora range office.

Burapahar (Ghorakati Range) :

  • Burapahar covers an area of approximately 16 kilometers for the Jeep Safari.
  • The Burapahar range office is very close to National Highway approximately 4 to 6 meters.
  • In Burapahar, within 15 kilometers before and 20 kilometers ahead, you will find approximately 1 to 2 resorts located near the Kohora range office.

Agaratoli Range :

  • Agaratoli covers an area of approximately 23 kilometers for the Jeep Safari.
  • The Agaratoli range office is located approximately 6 to 7 kilometers from the Naitonal Highway.
  • In Agaratoli, within 15 kilometers before and 10 kilometers ahead, you will find approximately 1 to 2 resorts located near the Agaratoli range office.
