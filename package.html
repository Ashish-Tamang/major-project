Kaziranga Tourism Packages :

Welcome to the Packages section for Kaziranga National Park. Here, tourists can book jeep safaris and elephant rides by exploring the available packages. We offer packages in four ranges: Bagori (Western Zone), Kohora (Central Zone), Agaratoli (Eastern Zone), and Burapahar (Ghorakati Zone). For the elephant safari, we have two zones: Bagori (Western Zone) and Kohora (Central Zone). All our packages for Kaziranga National Park are managed according to the guidelines of the Kaziranga Forest Department. We prioritize safety by strictly following the rules and regulations of Kaziranga.

    • Bagori(Western Range) :

    The Bagori Ecotourism Range is in the western part of Kaziranga National Park and is known as the best zone for wildlife sightings. This area has a beautiful landscape filled with greenery and a variety of wildlife. Jeep safaris in the Bagori zone offer great chances to see rhinoceroses, elephants, swamp deer, buffalo, and other animals in their natural environment. Both Jeep and Elephant safaris are available here, organized by the park authorities. The entry gate to this zone is in Bagori village, where registered Jeeps start their safari tours.

    Kohora(Central Range) :

    The Central Zone of Kaziranga National Park is the main area for ecotourism, located near Kohora village, about 2 km from the highway. It offers rich wildlife, diverse landscapes, and is great for birdwatching. Jeep safaris are held twice daily, providing opportunities to see one-horned rhinoceroses, elephants, and other wildlife, but visitors cannot get out of the vehicles.

    Burapahar(Ghorakati Range) :

    The Burapahar Tourism Zone of Kaziranga, about 40 km from the Central Zone, is the first zone accessible when traveling from Guwahati. Entry is through Ghorakati village along the National Highway. This zone offers Jeep safaris and trekking, appealing to bird watchers and nature enthusiasts due to its hilly terrain and dense greenery. Notable wildlife includes the Hoolock Gibbon, Capped Langur, and various bird species.

    Jeep safaris run daily from 7:00 AM to 4:00 PM, and visitors must follow park rules, such as staying in vehicles and being escorted by armed guards. Safaris and tour packages can be booked through our website.

    Agaratoli(Eastern Range) :

    The Agaratoli Range, located on the eastern side of Kaziranga National Park near Agaratoli village, is popular for its untouched beauty and diverse wildlife. It's a favorite spot for adventure enthusiasts and wildlife photographers. Visitors can spot rhinoceroses, wild buffalo, and elephants during Jeep safaris, which are the only way to explore this zone, as there are no elephant safaris available.

    Central Range :

    The Central Range (Kohora Range) starts at Mihimukh riding tower. Guests can enjoy a one-hour elephant safari through Dagland and Foliomari areas. The safari route is: Mihimukh → Kathpora → Daphlang → Diffolu River Bank → Mona Beel → Karasing → back.

    Western Range :

    The Western Range (Bagori) starts at the Bagori headquarters. Guests often choose this zone for close views of one-horned rhinos. The safari route is: Ongabeel → Rouman → Rajapukhuri → Monabeel → back.

    • Rules and Regulations To Know Before Head :

    • Elephant safaris offer great chances to see the one-horned rhino.
    • Safaris start at 5:00 AM, the best time for sightings.
    • Kohora Safari Zone is accessible only to foreigners and VIPs on elephants.
    • Indian nationals can choose the Bagori Safari Zone for elephant rides.
    • Safari timings and routes are set by forest officials.
    • The park authority can change timings and routes.
    • Don’t assume one safari zone is better than another.
    • All zones offer a deep experience of Kaziranga National Park.
    • Each zone is unique in forests, grasslands, wildlife, and scenery.
    • Elephants are four-seaters, with four tourists allowed per ride.
    • Groups of 10 elephants enter the safari zone with two forest guards.
    • Elephant safaris start at the Bagori or Western Zone gate.
    • Elephants are well-trained by Kaziranga forest authorities.
    • Book in advance due to limited availability of elephant seats.
